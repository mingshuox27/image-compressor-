Frequently Asked Questions

Is this image compressor really free?
Yes, completely free with no limits. Compress as many images as you want.
Are my images uploaded to your server?
No. All processing happens locally in your browser. Your images never leave your device.
What image formats are supported?
We support JPEG, PNG, and WebP formats. You can also convert between formats during compression.
Will I lose image quality?
The compression is lossy but adjustable. Use the quality slider to find the perfect balance. At 80-90% quality, the difference is usually unnoticeable.
Is there a file size limit?
For best performance, we recommend images under 20MB. Very large images may take longer to process.
